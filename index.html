10pm 10pm

2021-05-15

Ten pm, ten pm
Read my mind, read my mind
Galvanizing arm tranquilized
In delirium , a wilful distortion
you may accept it for what it’s worth.
A process to alleviate the self-imperfection, a beautiful parabola.
Thus, launch an hour into space, immense time, thou, cold and stern
LIGHT FOR ME.
